SUMMARY OF INJURIES
----------------------------------------------------
Service member killed in action (KIA). Injuries
sustained during combat patrol when vehicle struck
by improvised explosive device (IED). Pronounced
dead at field medical station by attending combat
medic and battalion surgeon.

Injuries included massive polytrauma with:
- Severe blast injuries to torso and extremities
- Traumatic brain injury
- Injuries incompatible with life

Death was immediate. No resuscitation attempted
at point of injury due to severity of wounds.
